Makalohe Hawai'i

Create a mobile app for tourists to use—while taking pictures—to learn, record, and explore the places they visited in Hawai’i and share on social media.

Challenge

Objective

Create an app to explore the islands and learn about the history in real-time.

Process

Planning

I set up the concept then created site map, style tile, lo and hi wireframe, logo and icon.

Solution

Final touches

Design an app that engages audiences starting from a photo taken by them, while providing a simple step-by-step process to access further information about the location.
